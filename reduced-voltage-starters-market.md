<p><p>Reduced voltage starters are critical components used in electric motor control, allowing for a softer start of induction motors. By reducing the voltage supplied to the motor at startup, these starters minimize inrush current, reducing mechanical stress and extending the lifespan of both the motor and the associated machinery. Common types include autotransformer starters, star-delta starters, and part-winding starters, each providing unique benefits in specific applications.</p><p>The Reduced Voltage Starters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.41% during the forecast period.
