<p><p>Smart water monitoring devices comprise several market types including Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) water meters, which enable two-way communication and real-time data analysis. Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) water meters provide one-way data transmission to utilities for efficient billing and monitoring. Communication network meters utilize various technologies, like IoT and cellular networks, to facilitate data transfer. Additionally, the “Other” category includes specialized devices that offer unique features for specific applications, enhancing overall water management and conservation efforts.</p></p>
