<p><p>Environmental consulting services encompass three main market types: Environmental Due Diligence, Environmental Site Assessment, and Environmental Audit. Environmental Due Diligence involves evaluating properties for contamination risks, often during real estate transactions. Environmental Site Assessment focuses on identifying potential environmental liabilities through systematic evaluations of a site's history and current conditions. Environmental Audits assess compliance with environmental regulations and standards, helping organizations understand their environmental impact and mitigate risks. Together, these services support sustainable development and responsible resource management.</p></p>
