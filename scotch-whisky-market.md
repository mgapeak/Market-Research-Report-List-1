<p><p>The Scotch whisky market comprises several key types. **Bottle Blended** whisky includes a mix of malt and grain whiskies bottled for consumer sale. **Bulk Blended** whisky is produced in large quantities for blending and aging, often sold in bulk to bottlers. **Single Malt** whisky is made from malted barley at a single distillery. **Bottle Single/Blended Grain** whiskies are either pure grain or a blend of grain whiskies. The **Other** category encompasses specialty whiskies, including those with unique aging processes or flavor profiles.</p></p>
