<p><p>The Distributed IP Camera market comprises two main types: Fixed Dispersed IP Cameras and PTZ-Type Distributed IP Cameras. Fixed Dispersed IP Cameras are stationary units designed for specific monitoring zones, providing high-resolution images for security and surveillance. In contrast, PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) Distributed IP Cameras offer adjustable movement capabilities, allowing operators to pan across a wide area, tilt for vertical view, and zoom in on specific subjects, thereby enhancing surveillance flexibility and coverage in various environments.</p></p>
