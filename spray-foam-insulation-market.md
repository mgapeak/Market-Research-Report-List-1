<p><strong>The Spray Foam Insulation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Cell Foam</li><li>Closed-Cell Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray foam insulation market is divided into two main types: open-cell foam and closed-cell foam. Open-cell foam is lightweight, flexible, and provides excellent sound insulation, making it suitable for interior applications and creating a soft barrier against air leakage. Closed-cell foam, on the other hand, is denser and provides superior insulation and moisture barrier, making it ideal for exterior applications and areas requiring structural support. Both types offer energy efficiency benefits and contribute to overall building performance.</p></p>
