<p><p>The anti-creasing agents market encompasses two primary types: dye-bath lubricants and wet processing lubricants. Dye-bath lubricants are added during textile dyeing to minimize creasing, ensuring a smooth finish and enhancing color uniformity. Wet processing lubricants, on the other hand, are utilized in various textile production stages to reduce friction and prevent damage, thereby improving fabric handling and quality. Both types effectively enhance the overall efficiency and quality of textile processing, contributing to better end-product performance.</p></p>
