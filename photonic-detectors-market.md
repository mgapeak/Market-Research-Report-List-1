<p><ul><li>Photoconductive Detector</li><li>Photovoltaic Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photonic detectors market encompasses various types, primarily photoconductive and photovoltaic detectors. Photoconductive detectors operate by changing their electrical conductivity in response to incident light, making them suitable for applications like fiber optics and spectroscopy. In contrast, photovoltaic detectors generate a voltage when exposed to light, functioning similarly to solar cells and excelling in energy harvesting applications. Both types serve critical roles in diverse industries such as telecommunications, environmental monitoring, and radiation detection, driving innovation in sensor technology.</p></p>
