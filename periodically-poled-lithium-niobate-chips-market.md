The market is segmented into three types: </p><p>1. **Single Grating**: These chips have one periodic domain structure, allowing for specific wavelength conversion. </p><p>2. **Multi-grating**: Featuring multiple gratings, they enable complex frequency conversion functions, enhancing versatility. </p><p>3. **Fanout Grating**: These chips employ a fanout design, distributing wavelengths over broader ranges, suitable for applications requiring multiple outputs. Each type caters to different optical needs and applications.</p></p>
