<p><p>The stationary air compressors market includes three primary types. Positive-displacement compressors operate by trapping a fixed volume of air and compressing it, commonly seen in piston and rotary screw designs. Centrifugal compressors use high-speed rotating impellers to increase air velocity and convert it into pressure, suitable for large-scale applications. Additionally, the "other" market encompasses various niche compressor types, such as diaphragm and scroll compressors, which cater to specific industrial needs, providing diverse options for compressed air solutions.</p></p>
