<p><p>Programmable Automation Controllers (PACs) are advanced industrial control systems that combine features of programmable logic controllers (PLCs) and PCs. The market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Integrated Type**: These PACs offer a compact design with built-in I/O, ideal for smaller applications needing tight integration. </p><p>2. **Distributed Type**: These utilize multiple PACs across a network for large-scale operations, allowing flexible configurations and scalability. </p><p>3. **I/O Type**: Focused primarily on providing modular I/O capabilities, these PACs enhance adaptability for specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
