<p><p>Web conferencing tools are primarily categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based tools are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access them via the internet, enhancing flexibility and scalability. They typically require subscription fees and are favored for their ease of use and low maintenance. In contrast, on-premise solutions are installed locally on a company’s servers, offering complete control over data and security. They demand significant upfront investment and ongoing IT support, making them suited for specific organizational needs.</p></p>
