<p><p>The Automotive AHSS market comprises several key types, each with unique properties. Dual Phase Steel offers a balance of strength and ductility, ideal for forming complex shapes. Martensitic Steel provides high strength and hardness, making it suitable for safety-critical components. Boron Steel is enhanced with boron to improve hardenability, perfect for structural applications. TRIP Steel (Transformation-Induced Plasticity) combines strength with formability, allowing for lightweight designs in vehicle manufacturing. Together, these materials enhance vehicle performance and safety.</p></p>
