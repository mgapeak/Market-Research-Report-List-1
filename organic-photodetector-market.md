<p><ul><li>Photon Based Organic Photodetector</li><li>Thermal Organic Photodetector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic photodetector market is primarily categorized into photon-based and thermal organic photodetectors. Photon-based organic photodetectors utilize light-sensitive organic materials to convert light into electrical signals, offering advantages like flexibility and lightweight design, ideal for applications such as imaging and communication. In contrast, thermal organic photodetectors detect variations in temperature caused by absorbed light, enabling effective sensing in low-light conditions. Both types are increasingly utilized in various applications, ranging from consumer electronics to environmental monitoring and industrial sensing.</p></p>
