<p><p>Intracranial Pressure (ICP) monitors are essential for assessing and managing conditions affecting brain pressure. The market is divided into invasive and non-invasive types. Invasive monitors involve techniques like ventricular catheters or subdural screws, providing direct pressure readings but requiring surgical intervention. Conversely, non-invasive monitors utilize methods such as transcranial Doppler or optical technologies, allowing for safer, simpler pressure assessments without penetration. Both types are crucial for effective brain injury management and neurological health monitoring in clinical settings.</p></p>
