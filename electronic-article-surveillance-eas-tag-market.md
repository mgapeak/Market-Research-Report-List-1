<p><p>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) tag market comprises various types, primarily hard tags, soft tags, and permanent deactivation tags. Hard tags are robust, reusable devices typically affixed to high-value items, offering strong security against theft. Soft tags are flexible, adhesive labels used on less expensive merchandise, providing a discreet solution. Permanent deactivation tags are designed to be disabled at the point of sale, ensuring items are secure until purchased, which enhances the overall efficiency of retail loss prevention strategies.</p></p>
