<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Accelerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Accelerometer</li><li>Pendulous Accelerometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric accelerator market encompasses various types, primarily linear and pendulous accelerometers. Linear accelerometers utilize piezoelectric materials to measure acceleration forces along a single axis, providing precise data for applications in automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors. Conversely, pendulous accelerometers utilize a pendulum mechanism, allowing them to measure acceleration in multiple directions by leveraging gravitational forces and inertia. Both types are crucial for motion detection and monitoring, finding widespread use in navigation, robotics, and structural health monitoring systems.</p></p>
