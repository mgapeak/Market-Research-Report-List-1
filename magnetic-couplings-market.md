<p><strong>The Magnetic Couplings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disc-type Coupling</li><li>Synchronous Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic couplings market is primarily categorized into two types: disc-type couplings and synchronous couplings. Disc-type couplings utilize magnetic forces to transmit torque between two aligned shafts, allowing for misalignment compensation and reduced wear. Synchronous couplings, on the other hand, synchronize the operation of connected components through magnetic fields, ensuring precise speed and torque transmission. Both types are increasingly favored in industries requiring high efficiency, low maintenance, and the ability to operate in harsh environments without mechanical contact.</p></p>
