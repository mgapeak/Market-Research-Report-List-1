<p><p>The aerial work platform market includes several types of equipment designed for elevating workers and materials. Boom lifts, characterized by extendable arms, provide access to high and hard-to-reach areas. Scissor lifts, with their crisscrossing support structure, offer stable vertical elevation for indoor and outdoor tasks. Vertical mast lifts are compact, providing minimal footprint access for jobs in tight spaces. Other platforms may include telescopic handlers and atrium lifts, catering to various specialized applications across industries such as construction, maintenance, and warehousing.</p></p>
