<p><p>The UV-curable resin market is primarily divided into two main types: non-acrylates and acrylates. Non-acrylates, which include oligoamines, are known for their superior adhesion and flexibility, making them suitable for high-performance coatings and adhesives. On the other hand, acrylates are widely used due to their rapid curing properties and durable finish, commonly found in printing inks, coatings, and varnishes. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, electronics, and packaging, each offering unique benefits for diverse applications.</p></p>
