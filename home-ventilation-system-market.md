<p><strong>The Home Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Ventilation Systems</li><li>Supply Ventilation Systems</li><li>Balanced Ventilation Systems</li><li>Energy Recovery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home ventilation system market includes various types designed to improve indoor air quality. Exhaust ventilation systems remove stale air and pollutants from inside, creating a negative pressure that draws in fresh air. Supply ventilation systems, conversely, introduce fresh air, ensuring a consistent flow. Balanced ventilation systems combine both exhaust and supply, maintaining air pressure equilibrium. Energy recovery systems enhance efficiency by transferring heat or humidity between outgoing and incoming air, reducing energy costs while ensuring optimal indoor environments.</p></p>
