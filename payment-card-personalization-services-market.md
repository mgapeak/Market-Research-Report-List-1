<p><ul><li>Dye Sub</li><li>Retransfer</li><li>Inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Payment Card Personalization Services encompass various technologies for customizing payment cards. Dye Sublimation uses heat to transfer dye onto the card surface, providing vibrant colors and images. Retransfer printing, on the other hand, involves printing on a film that is then applied to the card, offering higher resolution and durability, ideal for complex designs. Inkjet technology utilizes ink to print directly onto cards, allowing for quick changes and lower setup costs, making it suitable for smaller batches and on-demand customization.</p></p>
