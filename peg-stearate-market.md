<p><p>PEG stearate comes in various types characterized by their HLB (Hydrophilic-Lipophilic Balance) numbers, which affect their emulsifying properties. The 7.9 HLB type is suitable for water-in-oil emulsions, while the 8.5 HLB type is slightly more hydrophilic, aiding in stabilization. The 11.6 HLB type is well-suited for balanced systems, whereas the 13.6 and 14.6 HLB types are more hydrophilic, ideal for oil-in-water emulsions. The 15.6 and 17.3 HLB types emphasize stronger emulsifying capabilities for high-water formulations. There are also other forms catering to specific applications.</p></p>
