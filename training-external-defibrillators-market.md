<p><strong>The Training External Defibrillators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Training External Defibrillators market is primarily divided into two types: automatic and manual. Automatic defibrillators, also known as AEDs (Automated External Defibrillators), are designed to analyze the heart's rhythm and provide an electric shock if necessary without operator intervention, making them user-friendly. In contrast, manual defibrillators require trained personnel to analyze the rhythm and administer shocks, offering more control but necessitating advanced skills. Both types play critical roles in emergency response training and improve survival rates in cardiac emergencies.</p></p>
