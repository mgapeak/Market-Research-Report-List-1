<p><p>The adaptive optics market utilizing wavefront sensors comprises various types, including the wavefront curvature sensor and pyramid wavefront sensor. Wavefront curvature sensors measure the shape of wavefronts by assessing the curvature of incoming light, enabling precise real-time adjustments. Pyramid wavefront sensors, on the other hand, use a pyramid-shaped beam splitter to detect wavefront distortions, providing high-speed feedback for corrective actions. Other wavefront sensor types also exist, diversifying the applications and enhancing image quality in fields like astronomy, ophthalmology, and telecommunications.</p></p>
