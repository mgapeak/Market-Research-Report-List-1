<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems for diabetics are advanced tools designed to provide real-time glucose level data. The market is primarily divided into two types: wearable and implantable. Wearable CGMs, such as patches or sensors, are typically placed on the skin and can be easily removed and replaced. Implantable CGMs are surgically inserted under the skin for long-term use, offering a more stable glucose monitoring option with fewer necessary replacements, enhancing user convenience and data accuracy.</p></p>
