<p><strong>The Resistor Cooling Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Fan</li><li>Centrifugal Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resistor cooling fan market comprises two main types: axial fans and centrifugal fans. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, providing efficient airflow for cooling resistors in compact spaces. They are ideal for applications requiring direct cooling and lower pressure drops. In contrast, centrifugal fans draw air in at the axis and expel it at a right angle, generating high pressure and airflow, making them suitable for larger systems where enhanced cooling performance and air movement are necessary.</p></p>
