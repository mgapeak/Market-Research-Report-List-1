<p><p>The metal processing lubricant market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Quenching lubricants are used in heat treatment processes to cool metals rapidly, ensuring desired hardness and structure. Cutting lubricants facilitate machining operations by reducing friction and heat, improving tool life and surface finish. Grinding lubricants minimize heat generation and wear during grinding operations, enhancing efficiency and precision. Corrosion preventives protect finished metal products from rust and degradation, extending their lifespan and maintaining performance through protective coatings.</p></p>
