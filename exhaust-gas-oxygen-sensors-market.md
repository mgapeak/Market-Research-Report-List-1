<p><strong>The Exhaust Gas Oxygen Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Titanium Oxide Type</li><li>Zirconia Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Oxygen Sensors market comprises primarily two types: Titanium Oxide and Zirconia types. Titanium Oxide sensors operate on the principle of electrical conductivity variations in response to oxygen levels, offering a quicker response time and a broad measurement range. Zirconia sensors, on the other hand, rely on the ionic conductivity of zirconium dioxide, typically functioning at elevated temperatures for precise oxygen detection in exhaust gases. Both types are crucial for optimizing combustion efficiency and reducing emissions in automotive applications.</p></p>
