In contrast, electrical valves utilize electronic control for precise regulation, offering faster response times and improved efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in reducing nitrogen oxide emissions and enhancing engine performance, contributing to stricter environmental regulations and advancements in automotive technology.</p></p>
