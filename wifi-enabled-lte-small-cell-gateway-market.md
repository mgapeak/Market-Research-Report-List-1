<p><p>The WiFi Enabled LTE Small Cell Gateway market encompasses three main types: femtocells, microcells, and picocells. Femtocells are compact devices designed for home or small office use, enhancing indoor cellular coverage. Microcells cover larger areas, often deployed in urban settings to manage high traffic volumes. Picocells, smaller than microcells, provide targeted coverage in specific locations such as airports or hotels. These small cells help improve network capacity, reduce latency, and cater to the growing demand for mobile data.</p></p>
