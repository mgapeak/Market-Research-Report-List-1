<p><p>The COVID-19 detection kits market is primarily divided into two types: nucleic acid detection kits and antibody detection kits. Nucleic acid detection kits, such as PCR tests, identify the virus's genetic material, offering immediate results regarding active infections. In contrast, antibody detection kits assess the immune response by measuring antibodies produced after infection, indicating past exposure to the virus. Both types are essential for surveillance, diagnosis, and understanding the spread of COVID-19, contributing to effective public health strategies.</p></p>
