<p><p>The flexible mechanical couplings market can be segmented into three main types: sleeve, flanged, and clamped couplings. </p><p>Sleeve couplings are straightforward designs that connect two shafts using a cylindrical sleeve, allowing for slight misalignment. </p><p>Flanged couplings feature flanged ends that provide enhanced torque transmission and are easily detachable for maintenance. </p><p>Clamped couplings utilize clamps to secure the connection, enabling quick assembly and disassembly while accommodating moderate misalignment. Each type serves unique applications across various industries, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
