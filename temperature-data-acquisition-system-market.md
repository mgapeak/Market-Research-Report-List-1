<p><p>Temperature Data Acquisition Systems (DAS) are categorized into three primary types: benchtop, portable, and DIN Rail. Benchtop DAS is designed for laboratory settings, offering high accuracy and stability for extensive data collection. Portable systems are lightweight and battery-operated, making them ideal for field measurements and on-site applications. DIN Rail systems are compact and intended for installation in control panels, providing easy integration into existing infrastructure. Each type serves distinct environments and user needs, enhancing temperature monitoring efficiency.</p></p>
