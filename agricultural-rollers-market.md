<p><p>The agricultural rollers market is segmented into hydraulic and non-hydraulic rollers. Hydraulic rollers utilize hydraulic systems for improved efficiency and ease of use, allowing for adjustable pressure and speed while compacting soil or leveling land. They are favored for their versatility in varying terrain. Non-hydraulic rollers, on the other hand, rely on mechanical force and are generally simpler in design. These rollers are often used for specific applications where cost-effectiveness and straightforward operation are prioritized over advanced features.</p></p>
