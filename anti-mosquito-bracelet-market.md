<p><p>The anti-mosquito bracelet market is primarily divided into two types: chemical-based and ultrasound-based solutions. Chemical anti-mosquito bracelets release active ingredients, such as citronella or DEET, to repel insects through evaporation, providing a physical barrier against bites. In contrast, ultrasound bracelets emit high-frequency sound waves designed to deter mosquitoes by disrupting their navigation and communication. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences for safety, effectiveness, and environmental impact, contributing significantly to the overall market growth and innovation.</p></p>
