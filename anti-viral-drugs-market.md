<p><strong>The Anti-Viral Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nucleotide Polymerase Inhibitor</li><li>Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitor</li><li>Protease Inhibitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-viral drugs market comprises several key types, including nucleotide polymerase inhibitors, reverse transcriptase inhibitors, and protease inhibitors. Nucleotide polymerase inhibitors disrupt viral replication by targeting the enzyme responsible for synthesizing viral RNA or DNA. Reverse transcriptase inhibitors block the reverse transcription process, crucial for retroviruses like HIV. Protease inhibitors inhibit viral proteases, enzymes necessary for processing viral proteins, ultimately preventing the maturation and replication of viruses. These categories play vital roles in treating various viral infections.</p></p>
