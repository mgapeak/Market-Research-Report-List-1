<p><p>The traditional palletizer market is categorized primarily into High Infeed and Low Infeed palletizers. High Infeed palletizers handle products that come in from elevated conveyor systems, making them suitable for high-speed operations in industries like beverage and food processing. Conversely, Low Infeed palletizers are designed for products arriving at ground level, ideal for less demanding environments or smaller production lines. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the specific logistics, space constraints, and production rates of a facility.</p></p>
