<p><strong>The Aircraft Heat Exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plate-fin</li><li>Flat Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft heat exchanger market consists of two primary types: plate-fin and flat tube heat exchangers. Plate-fin heat exchangers utilize a series of thin plates to enhance heat transfer efficiency, making them compact and lightweight, ideal for aeronautical applications. Flat tube heat exchangers, on the other hand, employ flat tubular designs that provide a larger surface area for cooling or heating, promoting effective thermal management. Both types are critical for optimizing aircraft performance and maintaining operational efficiency in various flight conditions.</p></p>
