<p><p>Tinnitus management devices primarily include sound masking devices, notched music devices, and hearing aids. Sound masking devices generate ambient noise to help mask the ringing in the ears, providing relief to patients. Notched music devices play specially designed music that filters out specific frequencies associated with tinnitus, promoting habituation. Hearing aids, while primarily for amplifying sound, can also assist tinnitus sufferers by enhancing background noise, thus distracting from the perception of tinnitus. Together, these devices cater to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
