<p><p>The multi-function thermal camera market is categorized into three main types based on wavelength: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave thermal cameras. Short-wave length thermal cameras excel in detecting high-temperature objects and are often used in industrial applications. Mid-wave length cameras are versatile, suitable for both industrial and surveillance uses, effectively capturing temperature variations in diverse environments. Long-wave length thermal cameras dominate in security and monitoring applications, as they are highly sensitive to heat emitted by living beings, making them ideal for night vision and wildlife observation.</p></p>
